Reducing Water Waste in Daily Life

Every drop counts. Start saving water today.

The Problem

Everyday activities like leaving the tap running or ignoring small leaks lead to enormous water waste. The average U.S. household loses nearly 10,000 gallons annually just from leaks (EPA, 2024).

Global Impact

Over 2 billion people live in areas with high water stress (UN Water, 2023). Meanwhile, Americans use about 100 gallons of water each day (USGS, 2024).

Simple Water-Saving Tips

  • Fix leaky faucets and pipes promptly
  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth or shaving
  • Use a bucket to catch shower water while it heats up
  • Water plants early in the morning or late in the evening
  • Install low-flow showerheads and toilets
  • Only run dishwashers and washing machines with full loads

Take the Challenge

Share one water-saving tip with your family and challenge yourself to save at least 10 gallons of water this week. You don’t have to do everything—just something. Start today.
